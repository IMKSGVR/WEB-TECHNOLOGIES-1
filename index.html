A Student's Budget Trip to the Yoga Capital of the World

The Journey Begins...

Hey everyone! I just got back from an amazing solo trip to Rishikesh, and I had to share the experience. As a student, I was looking for a budget-friendly but adventurous trip, and Rishikesh was the perfect choice. It's a town in the Himalayan foothills beside the Ganges River. For more official info, you can check out its Wikipedia page.

Top 5 Things I Did (You Should Too!)

There's so much to do, but here's my personal list of must-do activities:

  1. River Rafting: This was the highlight! A 16 km stretch of pure adrenaline. Absolutely unmissable!
  2. Visit Laxman Jhula & Ram Jhula: These are two iconic suspension bridges with incredible views.
  3. Cafe Hopping: Rishikesh has some of the coolest cafes with delicious food and chill vibes.
  4. Attend the Ganga Aarti: A beautiful and spiritual evening ceremony by the river.
  5. Beach Camping: Spending a night in a tent by the river under the stars was magical.

My Essential Packing Checklist

Packing light is key. Here's what I recommend:

  • Quick-dry t-shirts and shorts
  • A sturdy pair of sandals or floaters
  • Sunscreen and a cap
  • A reusable water bottle
  • A power bank (super important!)
  • A small first-aid kit
